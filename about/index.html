About AutoCuts

We turn one recording into every video you need to post.

AutoCuts is an AI video editor built for one specific job: helping talking-head creators publish without spending half their week in an editor.

What we make

AutoCuts is a web-based AI video editor for creators who record themselves talking to camera. You upload one raw recording — interview, podcast, vlog, explainer, founder update — and we produce three things from it:

  • A polished long-form edit with filler words removed, dead air trimmed, and motion graphics added where they help.
  • Captioned 9:16 vertical shorts pulled from the strongest moments, ready for TikTok, Instagram Reels, and YouTube Shorts.
  • A clean review-and-approve workflow so nothing publishes without your sign-off.

Why we built it

Most video editors are built around a timeline. Timelines are the right interface for assembling visuals — montages, music videos, narrative film. They are the wrong interface for a creator who records a 40-minute monologue and wants to publish it.

For talking-head video, the editing job is mostly mechanical: remove the ums, tighten the pauses, find the lines that work as shorts, reframe them for vertical, burn captions, export per platform. AI can do that work. What it cannot do is decide what your video is about, or whether the cut you wanted to make was right. So we built AutoCuts to do the mechanical work and leave the editorial decisions to you.

Who AutoCuts is for

AutoCuts works best for:

  • YouTubers who run educational, commentary, interview, or vlog channels.
  • Podcasters who film their show and need shorts plus a clean long-form cut.
  • Founders recording video newsletters, product updates, or behind-the-scenes content.
  • Creators whose primary content is one or two people talking to camera.

It is not built for B-roll-heavy montages, music videos, or complex multi-camera narratives. If the edit is your art, AutoCuts is not the right tool — and we say so on every comparison page.

How we work

Editorial content on this site — our blog, feature pages, and comparison guides — is written by the AutoCuts Editorial team. We try to be honest, especially in comparisons. We acknowledge where competing tools (CapCut, Descript, OpusClip, DaVinci Resolve) are the better fit, and we recommend them when they are.

Where we are

AutoCuts is built in California. Your uploads are processed on secure cloud infrastructure; raw and processed media are removed within 30 days of project deletion. We do not use private videos for AI training without explicit consent. Full details are on our privacy page.
