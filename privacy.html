Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 8, 2026

1. Introduction

At AutoCuts ("we", "our", or "us"), we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website (autocuts.ai) and use our AI video editing service.

2. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Name, email address, password, and billing information (processed securely via third-party payment providers).
  • User Content: Video files, audio files, transcripts, and metadata that you upload to our platform for processing.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use our service, including processing times, credit usage, interaction with editing tools, and rendering history.
  • Device Data: IP address, browser type, operating system, and diagnostic data to ensure the stability of our platform.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To Provide the Service: We process your video and audio files using artificial intelligence to generate transcripts, detect silence, and suggest edits.
  • Service Improvement: We analyze anonymized usage patterns to improve our AI models, processing speed, and user interface. We do not use your private video content to train public AI models without your explicit consent.
  • Account Management: To manage your credits, process payments, and provide customer support.
  • Security: To detect, prevent, and address technical issues and fraudulent activity.

4. Data Processing and Storage

Your User Content (videos and audio) is stored securely on cloud infrastructure (e.g., AWS, GCP). After a project is completed or deleted by you, we remove the raw and processed media files from our active servers in accordance with our data retention policies, typically within 30 days of project deletion. Transcripts and metadata may be retained for account history and billing compliance.

5. Third-Party Service Providers

We may employ third-party companies and individuals to facilitate our Service ("Service Providers"), including:

  • Cloud hosting providers (for file storage and processing)
  • AI transcription and analysis APIs
  • Payment processors (e.g., Stripe)
  • Analytics and crash reporting tools

These third parties have access to your Personal Data only to perform these tasks on our behalf and are obligated not to disclose or use it for any other purpose.

6. Security of Data

The security of your data is important to us. We implement industry-standard encryption in transit (HTTPS) and at rest. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your Personal Data, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location (such as the GDPR in Europe or CCPA in California), you may have the right to access, update, delete, or restrict the processing of your personal information. You can exercise these rights by contacting our support team or managing your account settings.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at legal@autocuts.ai.
