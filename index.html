Common questions

Frequently asked questions

What types of video does AutoCuts work with?

AutoCuts is built for talking-head and spoken-word video: YouTube videos, tutorials, course content, podcasts, founder updates, and any recording where someone is speaking to camera.

Do I need video editing skills?

No. AutoCuts handles all the editing automatically. It removes dead air, cleans noise, trims bad takes, and suggests motion. You just review and approve.

How long does it take to get a finished video?

Most videos go from raw upload to reviewable edit in under 10 minutes. Your review takes a few more minutes, then the final render is ready to publish.

Do I lose control over my edits?

Never. AutoCuts suggests every cut, cleanup, and animation, but nothing ships without your approval. You decide what stays, what goes, and what gets changed.

Can AutoCuts generate shorts from my video?

Yes. AutoCuts finds the strongest moments in your recording and turns them into vertical shorts with captions and motion, ready to post to TikTok, Instagram Reels, or YouTube Shorts. You review and approve every clip before anything publishes.

What happens to my uploaded videos?

Your videos are processed on secure cloud infrastructure. Raw and processed media are removed within 30 days of project deletion. We never use your private videos for AI training without consent.
