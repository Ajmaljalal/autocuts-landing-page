FAQ

What videos does AutoCuts work best for? +

AutoCuts is best for talking-head formats: tutorials, explainers, product demos, podcasts, course clips, team updates, and any repeated spoken-video workflow where cleanup and pacing matter.

Can I review cuts before export? +

Yes. That is the point of the EDL-style workflow. AutoCuts surfaces decisions so you can restore what should stay, keep natural pauses, and approve the final story before render.

Does AutoCuts replace a full editor like Premiere or CapCut? +

Not completely, and it does not need to. AutoCuts is a focused editor for spoken content. It wins when your bottleneck is repetitive cleanup, pacing review, and render-ready approval, not full cinematic post-production.

How do credits work? +

Credits are used for the processing and render workload. You can start with free credits, then increase usage as your publishing cadence or team volume grows.
