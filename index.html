Chapter 05 · Q & A

Questions,
answered.

What types of video does AutoCuts work with?

Talking-head and spoken-word video. YouTube videos, tutorials, course content, podcasts, founder updates, any recording where someone is speaking to camera.

Do I need video editing skills?

No. AutoCuts handles the editing automatically: dead air, noise, bad takes, motion. You review and approve.

How long from upload to finished video?

Most videos go from raw upload to reviewable edit in under 10 minutes. Review takes a few more. Then the final render is ready to publish.

Do I lose control over my edits?

Never. AutoCuts suggests every cut, cleanup, and animation. Nothing ships without your approval.

Can AutoCuts generate shorts from my video?

Yes. The strongest moments become vertical clips with captions and motion, ready for TikTok, Reels, and Shorts. You review every clip before anything publishes.

What happens to my uploaded videos?

Processed on secure cloud infrastructure. Raw and processed media are removed within 30 days of project deletion. We never use your private videos for AI training without consent.

Is AutoCuts free to try?

Yes. Ten free credits, no credit card. Upload your first video in 30 seconds.
